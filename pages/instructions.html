How to Play

  1. Begin the game by entering your name.
  2. From the rock, paper, scissor, lizard, Spock page, click on the image of your choice.
  3. The computer will pick it's choice.
  4. The score will be updated letting you know if you won, lost or if it was a draw.
  5. Continue playing. Have fun!
  6. The first to five points wins the round.
  7. Once the round is completed, the score will be saved.
  8. Navigate to the scoreboard to see how you've done compared to others.

What wins against what?

  1. Paper cover Rock
  2. Rock crushes Scissors
  3. Scissors cuts Paper
  4. Spock smashes Scissor
  5. Spock vaporizes Rock
  6. Paper disproves Spock
  7. Lizard poisons Spock
  8. Lizard eats Paper
  9. Rock crushes Lizard
  10. Scissors decapitates Lizard
